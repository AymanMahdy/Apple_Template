Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. It designs, manufactures, and markets a range of consumer electronics, software, and services.

Founding and Early History

Apple Inc. was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. Initially focused on personal computers, the company revolutionized the tech industry with the Apple I and Apple II.

Product Innovation

Apple's product lineup includes iconic devices like the iPhone, Mac, and iPad. Each product is known for its sleek design, powerful performance, and user-friendly interface.

Leadership and Vision

Tim Cook, Apple’s CEO since 2011, has maintained the company’s focus on innovation. Under his leadership, Apple has continued to thrive as a leader in the global tech market.

Software and Services

Apple’s ecosystem includes iOS, macOS, and cloud-based services like iCloud. This seamless integration enhances user experience across all Apple devices.
